Why register with BeanStack?

When you register your business and the coffees you stock with BeanStack, you open your cafe up to our users. The next time someone searches for a coffee that you stock, your store is shown on the map and the user is given a link to your online store.

It's a really easy way to let coffee lovers know who you are and where to find your coffee.
